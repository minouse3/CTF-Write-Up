### Introduction
This challenge is a continuation of the Ghost in The Inbox challenge. Now that we know Ryan Law Wai-kwong was the person arrested, we need to find out how old he was when he was arrested.

The flag should be in the format:
```
hpCTF{**}
```

### How to find his age?
It's very, very easy. You just need to Google "Ryan Law Wai-kwong arrest age" and you'll find it right away, which is 46.

![method-1](assets/method-1.png)

### Flag
```
hpCTF{46}
```
